Our Academic Programs

Comprehensive education combining Islamic knowledge with modern academics

Islamic Studies

Our Islamic Studies program provides comprehensive religious education, ensuring students develop a strong foundation in Islamic knowledge and practice.

Qur'an Memorization

Systematic memorization of the Holy Qur'an with proper Tajweed and understanding.

Hadith

Study of the sayings and actions of Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him).

Fiqh

Islamic jurisprudence covering worship, transactions, and daily life matters.

Arabic

Comprehensive Arabic language studies including grammar, vocabulary, and literature.

General Studies

Our general academic program ensures students receive quality education in core subjects, preparing them for further studies and future careers.

Mathematics

Comprehensive math curriculum from basic arithmetic to advanced algebra and geometry.

English

Language skills development including reading, writing, speaking, and comprehension.

Science

Biology, Chemistry, and Physics taught with practical experiments and demonstrations.

Social Studies

History, Geography, and Civics to understand our world and society.

Academic Stages

1

Foundation Stage

Introduction to basic Islamic teachings, Arabic alphabet, and fundamental academic skills. Ages 5-7.

2

Elementary Stage

Building on foundations with structured Qur'an memorization, Hadith studies, and core academic subjects. Ages 8-10.

3

Intermediate Stage

Advanced Islamic studies including Fiqh, deeper Arabic studies, and expanded general academics. Ages 11-13.

4

Advanced Stage

Preparation for higher education with comprehensive Islamic scholarship and academic excellence. Ages 14+.
