Our Staff

Meet our dedicated team of educators and administrators

Sheikh Ahmed Jallow

Principal

With over 20 years of experience in Islamic education, Sheikh Ahmed leads our institution with dedication and vision.

Ustadha Fatou Sarr

Qur'an & Islamic Studies Teacher

Specialized in Tajweed and Qur'an memorization, Ustadha Fatou has helped hundreds of students master the Holy Qur'an.

Ustadh Mamadou Bah

Arabic Language Teacher

Expert in Arabic grammar and literature, Ustadh Mamadou makes learning Arabic engaging and accessible.

Mr. Lamin Ceesay

Mathematics Teacher

Passionate about mathematics education, Mr. Ceesay helps students develop strong analytical and problem-solving skills.

Ms. Awa Njie

English Language Teacher

Dedicated to improving students' communication skills, Ms. Njie creates an engaging learning environment.

Mr. Ousman Jallow

Science Teacher

With a background in science education, Mr. Jallow brings scientific concepts to life through practical demonstrations.
